My Projects

Udemy: Covid-19 Test

Covid-19 Test with Cough Sound using AI

To prevent the COVID-19 infections spreading, lots of diagnosis test-kits have been developed all around the world. Diagnosis is important for the isolation of the infected people, preventing them from spreading the disease and is also important for the observation and study of the infection. Here, my goal is to build up an algorithm that can classify the cough sound profile of COVID-19 patients and to use this analysis as an early prognosis tool.

Bogazici Lung Acoustic Laboratory(LAL)

Analyzing respiratory sounds and detecting anomalies in them with intelligent algorithms has opened a new era for auscultation that has 250 years of history. Furthermore, wheezes and crackles as abnormal sounds are hot topics currently being researched by many researchers. I work on machine learning algorithms for detecting wheezes and crackles that are superimposed on vesicular sounds.
